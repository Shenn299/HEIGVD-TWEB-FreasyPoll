Presenter functionality

  • A presenter is the administrator of the pool room
  • He's authenticated by his Google, Github or Twitter account
  • He can create one or several poll room
  • He ask a question to auditors in a poll room
  • He can view the curent responses provided by auditors
  • He can close a question, then no more auditor could answer
  • He can delete a question, then question and results will be no more visible

    • Auditor functionality

  • Everybody who know the pool room name can enter to it and be an auditor
  • Auditor isn't authenticated
  • He can answer and change his response until question is opened
  • He can view the curent outcome

    • Poll room & question functionality

  • A poll room has a unique name and may be protected with a password
  • A question always has three possibilities of response
  • A question always has a single correct response
