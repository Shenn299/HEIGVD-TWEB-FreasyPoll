Why use me ?


Imagine that you present an interesting topic to an audience. Each auditor has a laptop and a network access.
At a time, you want interact more dynamically with them with an interactive poll. But, you know that some people won't dare raise their hands to answer the poll.
Freasypoll is a good solution to answer poll online anonymously, in addition to other functionality.

Auditor functionality

  • Everybody who know the pool room name can enter to it and become an auditor
  • Auditor isn't authenticated by the application
  • He can answer each question only once
  • He can view the curent results for each question that he answered

    • Presenter functionality

  • A presenter is the administrator of the pool room
  • He's authenticated by his Freasypoll, Google or Twitter account
  • He can create one or several poll rooms
  • He ask questions to auditors in a poll room
  • He can view the curent responses provided by auditors with charts
  • He can close a question, then no more auditor could answer
  • He can delete a question, then question and results will be deleted
  • He can change the poll room password

    • Poll room & question functionality

  • A poll room has a unique name and may be protected with a password
  • A question always has three possibilities of response
  • A question always has a single correct response
